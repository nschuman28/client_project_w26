Career Development Office

UMSI CDO

UMSI's Career Development Office works to provide for current students and alumni of the School of Information. Such resources available include resume development workshops, interview practice, and advising on searching for positions.

The Career Development Office's team of advisors is comprised of professionals who are here to help you every step of the way throughout your job search. Whether you're a current student looking for an internship or a recent graduate in need of career advice, CDO advisors will assist you.

If you're in need of assistance with your job search, want to prepare for an interview, or are in need of any sort of career advice please schedule an interview with one of our advisors: Talk to a Career Coach

CDO Services

  • Connect Students with Employers
  • Provide Real World Experience for UMSI Students
  • Organize Recruitment Events and Career Fairs
